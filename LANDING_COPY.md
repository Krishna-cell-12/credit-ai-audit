---

## Copy Philosophy

Developer-facing copy has to clear three bars that consumer copy doesn't:

1. **No bullshit.** Developers have finely tuned BS detectors. No vague claims. Every claim must feel provable or self-evident.
2. **Intellectual curiosity over fear.** Lead with the interesting data insight, not the scary cost number (though cost numbers work too).
3. **Respect their intelligence.** Don't over-explain. Trust that they can connect the dots from a sharp premise.

**Tone:** Direct. Slightly irreverent. Data-forward. Never salesy.

## Copy Don'ts

- ❌ Don't say "AI-powered" — that's meaningless in 2025
- ❌ Don't use "revolutionary" or "game-changing"
- ❌ Don't make claims without numbers (or without being able to substantiate them within 6 months)
- ❌ Don't over-explain the technology — sell the outcome
- ❌ Don't use passive voice ("savings can be found") — use active ("we find what you're wasting")
